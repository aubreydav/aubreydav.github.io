About Us

Here Comes the Bride was founded in Laguna Beach, California. Founder and designer Aubrey Davenport has always had a love for fashion design at a young age. She later went to Parsons School of Design in New York City to study fashion design and focused on wedding dress design post-graduation.

"Your wedding day is the happiest day of your life and what woman doesn't want to spend it in the beautiful white gown of their dreams?" says HCTB founder Aubrey Davenport. HCTB offers original, chic custom wedding dresses for women of all shapes and sizes! See the Contact tab above to request a fitting appointmnet.
